We are pleased to announce that Service Apps have been made available for Webex Contact Center. With Service Apps, mission critical Contact Center integrations can now be deployed on behalf of an organization instead of individual users. With a standard integration, if the authorizing user departs or updates their password, the integration will simply stop working.
