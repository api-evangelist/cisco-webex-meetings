Today, we’re proud to announce the debut of our newly converged Webex Contact Center and Webex Suite developer portal. We’ve focused on usability and discoverability and implemented the following key features: Unified Access : Developers no longer need to log into two different portals for Webex Suite and Contact Center, making it easier to access all necessary content in one place. Flexible Navigation : We now support a much more flexible and extensible navigation interface, meaning that information is grouped logically and is more easily discoverable and digestible.
