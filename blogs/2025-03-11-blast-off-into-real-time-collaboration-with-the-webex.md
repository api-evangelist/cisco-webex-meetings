I'm a huge fan of Jackbox games, and one of the things I love about their lobby system is how it allows users to customize their avatar and display name before the game starts. This feature makes the experience much more immersive and engaging. Webex Embedded Apps provide developers with the power to integrate seamless, real-time experiences directly within Webex Meetings.
