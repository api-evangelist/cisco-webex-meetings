There was a great update recently released for the Meeting APIs that enables hosts to setup live streaming of their meetings and webinars through various services, such as IBM Video Streaming , Facebook Live , Vbrik Rev , and YouTube Live . This feature is ideal to help your meetings reach a wider audience for product launches, company announcements, expert panel discussions and more.
